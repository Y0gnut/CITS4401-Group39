This stakeholder plays a central role in the review and decision-making process for research ethics applications. Their responsibilities include evaluating research proposals, assessing risks to participants, collaborating with other committee members, and ensuring compliance with ethical standards.The scenario highlights several uncertainties regarding how ethics committee members conduct reviews, communicate with others, and manage conflicts of interest. Therefore, selecting this stakeholder allows for deeper investigation into critical aspects of system behaviour, decision-making processes, and constraints. The insights gained from this interview will help refine system requirements, particularly those related to workflow, collaboration, and application evaluation.2.
